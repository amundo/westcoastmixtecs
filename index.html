Goals for this site

There are tens of thousands of speakers of Mixtec languages in Washington, Oregon, and California.

This site is a clearinghouse of resources for Mixtec speakers and people who serve them.

We plan to include materials on:

  • Native-authored literature
  • Resources for Medical Interpreters
  • Resources for K-12 Teachers

We also plan to share content from our own research on the documentation of Mixtec languages as spoken in the diaspora.

  • Linguistic analysis of recordings in Mixtec
  • Pedagogical materials for learning Mixtec
