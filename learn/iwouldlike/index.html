Formas más complicadas

Fácil: With sugar?

Más complicado: Would you like sugar?

Lo más complicado: Would you like sugar with that?

Worker: Can I help you?
Customer: I’d like a coffee, please. = I would like a coffee, please.
Worker: Okay! Would you like milk and sugar with that?
Demetrio: Yes, I’d like milk and sugar, thanks.
